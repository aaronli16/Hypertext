Earth-1124: The Vet

Not every hero wears a cape. On Earth-1124, Aaron Li wears scrubs — and a stethoscope.

From a young age, he was inseparable from his two labadors: Travis and Isabelle. They were his best friends, his adventure partners, his therapy through chaos. Until life hit — hard.

Max was lost in a car accident. Luna passed a year later after a battle with cancer. Aaron was there through it all — helpless, heartbroken, and angry at how little he could do.

That was the moment everything changed.

He made a silent promise: No one should have to lose their best friend too soon.

Aaron became a veterinarian. Not for prestige. Not for profit. But for purpose. He worked in shelters, clinics, mobile vans — wherever the need was greatest. His specialty? Critical cases. Lost causes. Dogs others gave up on.

He saved lives. Not just dogs — families. Every tail wag he brought back meant a kid smiled again, a lonely elder felt loved again, a home became whole again.

And he never stopped. He started a network of affordable animal clinics. He created a foundation to fund cancer treatments for pets. He gave humans more time with the creatures that loved them most.

In this universe, Aaron Li doesn’t build tech or perform on stages. He heals. He listens. He cares. And that, too, is a kind of superpower.
